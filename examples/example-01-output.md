### HOOK

You're 12 miles from the trailhead when your filter clogs. You've got half a liter left. The creek is right there — cold, clear, moving fast. You've read the articles. You know better. But you're also really, really thirsty.

This is where most people make a decision they regret for the next three days.

---

### THE PROBLEM

Backcountry water treatment hasn't changed meaningfully in 20 years. Pump filters clog. Squeeze filters freeze. Chemical tablets taste like a swimming pool. UV pens require batteries, two hands, and 90 seconds of focused stirring per liter — which nobody actually does correctly when they're tired, cold, and trying to make camp before dark.

The result: giardia, cryptosporidium, and norovirus infect an estimated 500,000 backcountry travelers every year in North America alone. Not because they didn't have a filter. Because the filter they had was too annoying to use correctly every single time.

Water treatment failure isn't an equipment problem. It's a user experience problem.

---

### THE SOLUTION

NightGuard clips to any bottle cap and purifies 1 liter in 45 seconds — hands-free. No pumping. No squeezing. No stirring. Fill your bottle, clip NightGuard on, walk for 45 seconds. Done.

UV-C light at 265nm eliminates 99.9999% of bacteria, viruses, and protozoa — including giardia and cryptosporidium — without chemicals, without filters, and without the ritual that makes every other treatment method a chore.

**Here's how it works:** NightGuard's UV-C LED activates automatically when submerged. The clip-on design keeps it in contact with water for the full treatment cycle, then auto-shuts off. One charge lasts 150 treatment cycles — roughly a 10-day trip treated twice daily. Charges via USB-C in 90 minutes.

You fill the bottle. NightGuard does the rest.

---

### WHY WE BUILT THIS

August 2021. I was 4 days into a 10-day loop in the Cascades when I got hit with giardia. I had treated the water — I thought. Turns out I'd rushed the treatment on a cold morning and cut the UV exposure time short.

I spent the next 72 hours in my tent, dehydrated, 9 miles from the nearest trailhead, wondering why the most important piece of safety gear in my pack was also the most likely to be used wrong.

I'm a wilderness EMT. I've treated a lot of backcountry illnesses. Water-borne illness is consistently the most preventable — and the most common. So I spent the next 18 months building what I wish I'd had.

NightGuard is the result.

---

### WHAT MAKES NIGHTGUARD DIFFERENT

**It works while you walk.** Every other UV treatment method requires you to stop, stir, and time. NightGuard clips on and treats while you're moving. You never slow down.

**No filters to clog, no cartridges to replace.** The UV-C LED lasts 10,000 cycles — roughly 15 years of regular use. There's nothing to replace. Ever.

**One-handed operation.** Fill the bottle. Clip. Walk. That's it. We designed for the moments when you're tired, cold, and just want water — not a treatment ritual.

**Works at altitude and in freezing temperatures.** UV-C performance doesn't degrade in cold water. Tested at 12,000 feet in 28°F stream water. Works.

**No chemical taste.** Zero. The water tastes like water.
