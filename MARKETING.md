## Target Buyers

### Primary: Product Creators (Kickstarter/Indiegogo)
- Hardware startups, consumer product inventors, tech gadget makers
- Median campaign goal: $25,000–$100,000
- Pain: Weeks spent on campaign copy; most publish generic, unconverted pages
- Willingness to pay: High (campaign success = $10K–$1M+ in revenue)

### Secondary: Nonprofits & Community Projects (GoFundMe)
- Charities, community groups, personal causes
- Pain: Don't know how to tell their story compellingly
- Volume: 250K+ new GoFundMe campaigns launched monthly

### Tertiary: Agencies & Freelancers
- Digital marketing agencies that build crowdfunding campaigns for clients
- Freelance copywriters who want a repeatable system
- This skill = their $2,000 deliverable, 10x faster

## Key Differentiators

1. **Platform-specific:** Kickstarter vs Indiegogo vs GoFundMe have different norms — this skill knows them
2. **Reward tier psychology:** Decoy pricing, early bird scarcity, superfan tier — no generic AI does this
3. **5-email sequence included:** Most campaigns send 1 backer update; this gives a full sequence
4. **Reddit post included:** Most campaigns ignore Reddit; it drives 10-30% of organic traffic for niche products
5. **Complete system:** Campaign page + rewards + emails + social = nothing left to figure out
