## What This Skill Does

Generates complete crowdfunding campaign copy for Kickstarter, Indiegogo, and GoFundMe — from the main campaign page to reward tier descriptions, backer update emails, and a full social media launch kit.

Crowdfunding success is 80% copywriting. Most campaigns fail not because the product is bad, but because the story doesn't convert. This skill structures your campaign using proven frameworks from $1M+ funded projects.

## Prompts (4 Total)

### Prompt 1: Campaign Page Copy
**Output:** Full campaign page — hook, story, problem/solution, product demo narrative, timeline, team credibility, FAQ (8 questions), and call to action  
**Length:** 1,000–1,500 words  
**Format:** Structured sections ready to paste into Kickstarter/Indiegogo campaign editor

### Prompt 2: Reward Tier Descriptions
**Output:** 6–8 backer reward tiers with names, price points, descriptions, and urgency triggers  
**Format:** Table + individual copy blocks per tier  
**Includes:** Early bird tier, main tier, premium tier, "superfan" tier, and exclusivity framing

### Prompt 3: Backer Update Emails
**Output:** 5-email sequence covering pre-launch, launch day, milestone celebration, shipping update, and thank-you/future offer  
**Format:** Subject line + body for each email  
**Includes:** Social proof hooks, urgency reactivation for lapsed backers, referral ask

### Prompt 4: Social Media Launch Kit
**Output:** Full social campaign across 5 platforms  
- LinkedIn: 3 posts (announcement, behind-the-scenes, final push)  
- Twitter/X: 10-tweet launch thread + 5 standalone tweets  
- Instagram: 3 caption + hashtag sets  
- Facebook: 2 posts (launch + milestone)  
- Reddit: 1 community-appropriate post for relevant subreddits

## Inputs Required

All prompts use a shared project brief:

```
PRODUCT/PROJECT NAME: [name]
WHAT IT DOES: [1-2 sentences]
WHO IT'S FOR: [target backer]
CAMPAIGN PLATFORM: [Kickstarter / Indiegogo / GoFundMe / other]
FUNDING GOAL: [$X]
CAMPAIGN DURATION: [X days]
ORIGIN STORY: [why you built this]
KEY FEATURES: [top 3-5 differentiators]
TEAM: [founder name + relevant credential]
EXISTING TRACTION: [prototypes, pre-signups, press, etc.]
TONE: [passionate / professional / playful / urgent]
```

---

## Output Quality Standards

- Opens with a hook that answers "why now, why you, why this"
- Problem framing uses sensory language (shows the pain, doesn't just name it)
- Solution section leads with transformation, not features
- Reward tiers use psychological pricing (charm pricing, decoy middle tier, FOMO on early bird)
- All social posts include platform-specific character count compliance
- FAQ anticipates the top 8 objections that kill crowdfunding conversions
